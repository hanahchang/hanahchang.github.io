Hanah Chang
Hanah is an extraordinarily average, country music loving, sitcom alcoholic and sports enthusiast who can be found watching the Chicago Bears, Chicago Blackhawks, Chicago Cubs, and the Chicago Bulls. The last time she cried was at rehearsal when the Bears got destroyed by the Packers. In her ideal world, everything would be made out of edible bread and time travel would be very very very possible so that she can kick it with the dinosaurs. Also, people would use the poop emoji more. Hanah is a little dyslexic (thank god her name is a palindrome ha ha) but actually enjoys reading and writing. Her favorite word is kerfuffle. When she is not drinking (hehe), eating or sleeping, Hanah can be found pretending to be an intergalactic space princess. And if the world was under a zombie-alien hybrid apocalypse and she could only save either all the babies or all the grown-ups, she would choose dogs. One day she hopes she can be the president of a third world country. Or a well-tipped bartender. It's a tossup. Hanah knows all the prime numbers up to the number 13.
